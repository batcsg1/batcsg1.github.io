First batch of Seeds

First month of the seedlings

After the first the month, the seedlings are going along pretty well. 7 seeds out of the original 9 from the first batch successfully germinated. As of the 12th of August, the seedlings are averaging 1 to 3cm tall. One of the seedlings had also developed two stems, which was very unusual. A sort of thing like this doesn't happen very often, especially during germination.

The following table contains data specifying the Names and height of each seedling in batch 'A' as of August 15th. All heights were recorded using a bamboo skewer marked with centimeter markings.
