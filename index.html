About this Website

This website delves into the journey of a dozen Monkey Puzzle seeds that I had collected from a place called Chingford Park, located in the city of Dunedin in New Zealand. Since June 9th, 2024, I have been nuturing 7 little Monkey Puzzle seedlings that have sprouted out of a batch of 9 seeds from the original dozen from Chingford Park. And since August 4th, 2024, I decided to sow 4 more seeds, after 2 out of the original 9 seeds died.
